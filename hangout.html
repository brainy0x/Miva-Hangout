What this is

A rooftop reset for Miva students

Miva Lagos Hangout is a first-of-its-kind meetup for Miva Open University students at the Lagos Study Centre — one afternoon, one rooftop, one small crowd. No lectures, no deadlines. Just food, music, games, and a chance to actually meet the classmates you've only seen in a discussion forum.

Because the rooftop can only hold 40 people, this one stays intimate on purpose — every ticket holder gets a seat, a plate, and room to actually talk to people.

FAQ

No. Only students with a confirmed ticket will be admitted. Registration closes automatically once all 40 tickets are claimed.

Yes — jollof rice & turkey, chin chin & doughnut, malt & bottled water are all covered by your ticket.

Refund and transfer details will be shared on the ticketing page once the platform is confirmed.
